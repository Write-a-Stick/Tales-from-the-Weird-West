<h1>Tales from the Weird West</h1>
</center>

Emma was content helping her dad run their ordinary little inn - at least until a charming stranger strolls in, pays with impossible money, and points her toward a world of wonder that’s been hiding in plain sight. She learns unwelcome truths about her family, truths burdened with rules she can’t break and promises she can’t keep. Home is no longer the place to be safe in - it becomes the place to protect.
